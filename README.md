# Kill Switch

**A resistance idle game against unchecked AI.**

[▶ Play now](https://vlad-codes.github.io/killswitch-idlegame/)

---

Year 2026. Frontier AI is scaling past every safety threshold — no democratic oversight, no accountability, no pause. You are building a global resistance movement. Reach **1 billion in resistance** to force a worldwide moratorium.

## How to play

Click the Kill Switch to gather resistance. Use it to build your movement and unlock breakthroughs. Production runs automatically — step away, come back, the fight continues.

## Features

- **Idle progression** — buildings generate resistance per second, upgrades multiply output
- **Breakthroughs** — 30+ upgrades that scale click power and production
- **Field Decisions** — scenario-based events where your choice affects production for 30 seconds
- **Phantom Nodes** — interceptable signal bursts that appear randomly on screen for a click power boost
- **Critical Moments** — catch the Kill Switch at peak intensity for a ×5 hit
- **Achievements** — 20+ milestones tracking your progress
- **Prestige** — reset with a permanent multiplier and go again
- **Offline progression** — up to 4 hours of passive gains while away

## Tech

Vanilla HTML, CSS, JavaScript. No framework, no build step, no dependencies. Save state via `localStorage`.
